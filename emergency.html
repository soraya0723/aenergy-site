GoodWe LVSMT Series

The GoodWe LVSMT Series is a three-phase inverter designed with low-voltage power input, making it ideal for commercial installations. Built to address South American market needs, it eliminates the need for costly transformers in systems above 10 kW, reducing installation and investment costs.

  • ⚡ Max. efficiency up to 98.8%
  • ⚡ String-level monitoring
  • ⚡ IP65 ingress protection
  • ⚡ Fuse-free design (lower O&M costs)
