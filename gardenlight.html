GoodWe ES Uniq Series

The GoodWe ES Uniq Series (3–12 kW) is designed for residential PV systems with seamless on-grid and off-grid compatibility. Supporting parallel connection of up to 6 inverters, it ensures flexible system expansion. The inverters feature a user-friendly touch-screen LCD and wide battery compatibility, including GoodWe’s Lynx A and Lynx U batteries.

  • ⚡ 3-in-1 Port: generator, smart loads, on-grid inverter
  • ⚡ Micro-grid operation with PV inverter
  • ⚡ Seamless switching to backup < 4ms
  • ⚡ AI-driven AFCI
  • ⚡ IP66 ingress protection
