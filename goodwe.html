GoodWe LVMT Series

The GoodWe LVMT Series is designed to meet the demands of medium and large-scale commercial PV systems in South America. With up to 180% DC oversizing and 110% AC output overloading, it delivers exceptional performance and profitability. Its compact design, power boost function, and full-load operation at 50ºC ensure reliability and faster return on investment.

  • ⚡ Up to 98.8% max efficiency
  • ⚡ 4 MPPTs for maximum flexibility
  • ⚡ String-level monitoring
  • ⚡ Power line communication (PLC)

Key Features

High Power Generation

Boost solar harvest with advanced oversizing capabilities and robust design.

  • Up to 180% DC input oversizing
  • 110% AC output overloading
  • Full-load running at 50ºC

Advanced MPPT Technology

Optimize energy yield under shading or mismatch conditions with four independent trackers.

  • 4 MPPTs for maximum output
  • Minimizes shading losses
  • 98.8% maximum efficiency

Reliable & Intelligent Monitoring

Ensures system reliability with advanced monitoring and communication features.

  • String-level monitoring
  • Power line communication (PLC)
  • Compact, transformerless design
