GoodWe RSD Receiver

The GoodWe RSD Receiver is a key component of the RSD 2.0 solution for PV systems, capable of connecting with one or two modules. Acting as a module-level rapid shutdown device, it enhances fire safety for solar rooftops and buildings by ensuring reliable shutdown during emergencies.

  • ⚡ Plug & Play for quick installation
  • ⚡ Integrated SoC for high reliability
  • ⚡ PLC-based communication
  • ⚡ NEC 2017/2020/2023 compliant & SunSpec certified
View Datasheet

Key Features

Enhanced Fire Safety

Provides rapid module-level shutdown for safe emergency response.

  • Complies with NEC 2017/2020/2023
  • Activates when PLC signal is lost
  • Supports external initiator

Reliable Operation

Ensures stable communication and continuous monitoring of module operation.

  • Integrated SoC for reliability
  • Supports PLC communication
  • Compatible with GoodWe inverters or external transmitters

Durable & Easy to Install

Compact, rugged design with simple plug-and-play installation.

  • Plug & Play setup
  • Ingress protection rating IP68 / UL Type 6P
  • Wide operating temperature −40 °C to +85 °C
