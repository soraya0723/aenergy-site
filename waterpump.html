GoodWe Lynx A Series Battery

The GoodWe Lynx A Series harnesses lithium iron phosphate (LFP) battery technology to deliver safe, reliable, and long-lasting energy storage. Designed for residential applications, it maximizes self-consumption, provides backup power, and ensures high energy density in a compact design. Its scalable architecture allows households to expand capacity as their energy needs grow.

  • ⚡ Reliable LFP technology with high cycle stability
  • ⚡ Compact, lightweight, and space-saving
  • ⚡ Expandable capacity for flexible applications
  • ⚡ Remote diagnosis & updates via inverter
  • ⚡ Smart auto-reboot after undervoltage

Key Features

Superb Safety & Reliability

Built with proven LFP cell chemistry to ensure long cycle life and safe operation.

  • High cycle stability
